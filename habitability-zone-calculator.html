§3.1 Equilibrium temperature

T_eq = T_star × √(R_star / (2 × d)) × (1 − A)^0.25

Where T_star is host star effective temperature (K), R_star is star radius (m), d is planet orbital distance (m), A is bond albedo (0–1). Earth's T_eq ≈ 255 K (well below freezing — the greenhouse fills the gap).

§3.6 Stellar habitable zone

HZ_inner = 0.95 × √(L_star / L☉) · HZ_outer = 1.67 × √(L_star / L☉)

For G2V (Sun-like) star, HZ ≈ 0.95–1.67 AU. M-dwarfs have very tight HZs (TRAPPIST-1: 0.022–0.032 AU).
